This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection and use of Personal Information. ## Personal Information ECG mob stores your personal information on your device and does not share it with anyone else, unless: * you explicitly share ECG with another application or send it by email; * you use ECG mob together with [Telepat Medsenger](https://apps.apple.com/ru/app/medsenger/id1174329869) and explicitly choose to share ECG (see next section for more details). ECG mob uses a microphone to record an ECG. The recording is never shared with anyone and is destroyed as soon as the PDF file is generated. ## Telepat Medsenger Integration (since ECG mob v1.8) Whenever you use ECG mob together with [Medsenger](https://apps.apple.com/ru/app/medsenger/id1174329869) application, you agree to share the following information with [https://medsenger.ru](https://medsenger.ru): * Your identifier in Medsenger app * Your birthdate * Your full name * Your gender Please review [Medsenger Privacy Policy](https://medsenger.ru/privacy) for details. *07/24/2022*
