Where can one buy

The «KardioMob» ECG monitor has not yet been mass-produced, but the NPF BIOSS firm (www.bioss.ru , www.cardio.bioss.ru ) has produced two devices that can also be used with "FA mob" and "ECG mob" applications, these are: ICRZ -1 (Fig. 1) and the “Heart” mini cardiograph (Fig. 2). They have the same functions as KardioMob, but structurally they are less convenient for the First Aid option and they do not have a sound pulse indication.
