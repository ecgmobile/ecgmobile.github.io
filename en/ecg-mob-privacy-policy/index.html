Privacy Policy

BIOSS built the ECG mob app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by BIOSS at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection and use of Personal Information.

Personal Information

ECG mob stores your personal information on your device and does not share it with anyone else, unless:

  • you explicitly share ECG with another application or send it by email;
  • you use ECG mob together with Telepat Medsenger and explicitly choose to share ECG (see next section for more details).

ECG mob uses a microphone to record an ECG. The recording is never shared with anyone and is destroyed as soon as the PDF file is generated.

Telepat Medsenger Integration (since ECG mob v1.8)

Whenever you use ECG mob together with Medsenger application, you agree to share the following information with https://medsenger.ru:

  • Your identifier in Medsenger app
  • Your birthdate
  • Your full name
  • Your gender

Please review Medsenger Privacy Policy for details.
