FIRST AID

First aid - assistance to victims provided by people around before the arrival of medical workers. First Aid actions are extremely important, as the time factor is of great importance, and the quick adoption of necessary measures by people around us can save many lives. The ability to provide first aid is considered necessary for a modern person. In many countries, this is taught in schools, colleges, universities. Moreover, persons whose profession is associated with an increased risk should possess these skills: military personnel, police, firefighters, rescuers, climbers, drivers, bodyguards, etc. There are a large number of companies that provide such training. Every year, tens of millions of people undergo First Aid training around the world. The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies coordinate this work. She also issues textbooks and instructions on how to conduct First Aid.

An important element of First Aid is cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR), with its providence, it is first necessary to determine the presence or absence of the victim's pulse. If there is no pulse, immediate cardiac massage is needed, if there is a pulse, massage is not only not necessary, but also dangerous. In accordance with the instructions, the pulse is determined by palpation of the carotid artery. However, practice and special studies have shown that in most cases, especially in extreme conditions, it is not possible to control the pulse of the injured person using this method. This fact significantly reduces the number of people saved during First Aid.

Personal ECG monitor "KardioMob" and smartphone with FA mob application solve the problem of reliably determining the presence or absence of cardiac activity in heart attacks, accidents, injuries, terrorist acts or on the battlefield. When applying the devices do not need to grope the victim's carotid pulse and that it is very important that you do not need to interrupt the heart massage and artificial respiration for the periodic control! Reliable information on pulse helps decide the feasibility of cardiopulmonary resuscitation. When using ECG monitor the actions of others in carrying out resuscitation until the arrival of medical professionals become more efficient.

Application FA mob is intended for use in conjunction with personal ECG monitor "KardioMob" or with similar parameters. Cardiac activity of the victim is evaluated by electrocardiogram (ECG), which is deducted from the electrodes glued to the patient's wrists; it allows not undressing a person, which is very important in an extreme situation. ECG with wrists corresponds to the first standard lead hand in hand. ECG monitor enhances the biopotencialy heart, encodes them and cable passes into the headphone socket your Smartphone or tablet, if such is not available, the connector with the cable signal adapter into the socket to charge your Smartphone. Surrounding people who provide first aid to the injured, receive the necessary information on Smartphone displays, a special algorithm determines the presence or absence of a pulse.

Cardiac rhythm control

In cardiac rehabilitation field after surgery

For patients who have had heart surgery (particularly operation RFA-radiofrequency ablation), for a better recovery requires daily monitoring of the heart. The use of "KardioMob" is an economical and effective solution to this problem. After the RFA "KardioMob" will help to monitor how effectively suppressed arrhythmia after operation do not manifest whether episodes of arrhythmias and, if so, what it is and how often. To identify rare and hidden arrhythmias who are adults, the system of "KardioMob" will help identify episodes of arrhythmias that occur at home. This applies to patients who are already diagnosed with arrhythmia and those who have suspected its existence. For patients experiencing periodic chest discomfort, shortness of breath, chest pains, will help identify the problem at an early stage.

To monitor the state of the heart after a myocardial infarction

Patients who have suffered myocardial infarction or severe heart attack, is very important for the day-to-day monitoring of the heart. "KardioMob" will help in this and reduce the probability of complications.

To monitor the actions of cardiac drugs

Patients receiving cardiac medication, "KardioMob" allows you to monitor their operation and efficiency, and enables you to remotely consult with a physician regarding maintenance or correction of drug therapy.

To monitor the status of patients from group risk

Nowadays many people older than 40 years are at risk of cardiovascular disease. "KardioMob" will help prevent serious complications, identifying the changes at an early stage of the process.

At improving physical education (fitness)

Monitoring ECG in the process of doing fitness is especially important for older people with certain chronic diseases. Inadequate physical activity can lead to malfunctions of the heart. Using «KardioMob», they can be prevented or detected at an early stage.
